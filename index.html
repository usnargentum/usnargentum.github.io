Autonomous Rescue Rover

About Us

Building a Rescue Rover from the Ground Up

USN Argentum is a student team at the University of South-Eastern Norway designing and building a fully autonomous rover to compete in RoboCup Rescue 2026.

Competition RoboCup Rescue 2026 — international autonomous robotics, disaster terrain navigation and victim detection without human control.
Navigation Autonomous SLAM-based mapping and navigation in unstructured environments.
Technology ROS, embedded systems, computer vision for victim detection and a custom mechanical platform.
Team 7 students across mechanical engineering, computer engineering and economics.
